This co-authored study applies electroencephalography to help assess the etiology of coma in children, with attention to retinopathy-negative cerebral malaria — a diagnostically challenging group. My contribution was on the EEG signal analysis and the evaluation of predictive models discriminating cerebral malaria from non-malarial cases.
