This co-authored study compared children with retinopathy-negative CM against both retinopathy-positive CM and nonmalarial coma controls, using EEG to assess whether retinopathy-negative CM resembles malarial or nonmalarial coma. My contribution focused on the machine-learning analysis of the EEG signals — including exploratory data analysis, feature selection and imputation, and training and evaluating ensemble classifiers (GLM, GAM, and Random Forest) with ten-fold cross-validation.
