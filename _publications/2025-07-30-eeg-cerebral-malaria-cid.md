This co-authored study uses electroencephalogram (EEG) features to distinguish cases of cerebral malaria from other causes of fever and coma in a cohort of Malawian children. My contribution focused on the machine-learning analysis of the EEG signals — including exploratory data analysis, feature selection and imputation, and training and evaluating ensemble classifiers (GLM, GAM, and Random Forest) with ten-fold cross-validation.
